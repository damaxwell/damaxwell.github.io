Calculus III is the most real-world of all the classes in the calculus sequence. Now we work in 3-dimensional space, with applications to physics and engineering throughout the curriculum. By the end of the course, we will have all covered all the tools needed to tackle topics such as mechanics, fluid dynamics, thermodynamics and Maxwell’s equations.
