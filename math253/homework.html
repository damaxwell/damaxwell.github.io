Homework Sets

All problems come from the course text unless otherwise noted.

  • Written homework guidelines:
    • Problem number is clearly stated at the beginning.
    • First section: Given. State the information you’ve provided.
    • Second section: Required. State the task you’ve been asked to perform.
    • Final section: Solution. Box your final answer.
    • Each new problem is below the previous one, or on a new page, and occupies the full width of the page.
    • Tidy handwriting. Lots of white space. Large graphs when problems request plots.

The solutions in Canvas adhere to these guidelines are a template for you to follow.
